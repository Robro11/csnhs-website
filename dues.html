Activity Fee:

Computer Science National Honor Society officially collects an Activity Fee that is paid through our Booster Club. We use these fees to pay for cords at graduation and other various Lamar-related essentials. Additionally, Activity Fees are used to purchase materials used in our projects, like the Hack-a-thon. Using the link below, please pay our current Activity Fee of $37. If you need assistance paying, please contact the officers or our sponsor, and Computer Science teacher, Ms. Sally Woolweaver.
